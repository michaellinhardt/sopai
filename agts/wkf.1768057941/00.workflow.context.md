### Key Findings Summary

**Global Options:**
- **Fastest solutions**: Merchant of Record services (Paddle, Lemon Squeezy) or Payoneer
- **Cheapest ongoing**: Georgia IE (1% tax) or Wyoming LLC
- **Most credible**: US LLC via Stripe Atlas or UK Ltd
- **EU-focused**: Estonian e-Residency or Irish Ltd

**Vietnam Options:**
- **Recommended for user's situation (USD 16,000 capital):** 100% Foreign LLC with DT4 visa (1-year, no TRC) or continue tourist visa while building revenue
- **If married to Vietnamese:** Joint Venture with spouse offers simpler path
- **No digital nomad visa exists** in Vietnam (proposed "Golden Visa" not yet implemented)
- **Software/SaaS sector:** 100% foreign ownership allowed, no minimum capital, but USD 10-20K recommended
- **Work permit exemption:** Available for investors with sufficient capital contribution
