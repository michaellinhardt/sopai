Help user understand what Ralph and BMAD do, their reliability, popularity, strengths, weaknesses, and community size to determine if either should be adopted.

   - 02: Deep dive on BMAD AI workflow

   - 04: BMAD vs other methods comparison
2. Synthesis phase (1 sub-agent):
   - 05: Read all reports, generate final recommendation

## Acceptance Criteria
- Comprehensive understanding of both methods
- Clear comparison with alternatives
- Actionable recommendation on adoption
