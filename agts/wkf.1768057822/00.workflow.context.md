# Workflow Context

## Initial Request
Research options for a French citizen living in Vietnam on a tourist visa to receive payments from a SaaS business.

## Situation Summary
- **Nationality:** French
- **Location:** Vietnam (tourist visa, renewed regularly)
- **French bank account:** None
- **French tax number:** None
- **Vietnamese bank account:** Exists, but eligibility uncertain after leaving previous employer
- **Goal:** Find ways to legally (or temporarily) receive subscription payments from a SaaS

## Workflow Purpose
Identify all possible methods to receive SaaS payments given the constraints, evaluate their feasibility, risks, and suitability.

## Method
1. Parallel research phase with 3 agents:
   - Legal options worldwide
   - Cross-the-line temporary solutions
   - Vietnam company formation options
2. Verification phase: validate each option with online sources
3. Q&A session: personalized assessment
4. Final synthesis with pros/cons

## Acceptance Criteria
- Comprehensive list of options
- Each option verified for feasibility
- Personalized Q&A completed
- Final results document with actionable recommendations
